1. Grilled Chicken Salad

Ingredients: Grilled chicken breast, lettuce, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, olive oil, lemon juice.

Benefits: High in lean protein, rich in vitamins, low in unhealthy fats.

  1. Grill chicken breast until fully cooked and slightly charred.
  2. Toss lettuce, tomatoes, and cucumber with olive oil & lemon juice.
  3. Slice chicken and place on top of salad. Serve fresh.

2. Paneer & Spinach Stir Fry

Ingredients: Paneer cubes, spinach, garlic, olive oil, black pepper.

Benefits: Great vegetarian protein source, packed with iron and calcium.

  1. Saut√© garlic in olive oil, add paneer cubes and cook until golden.
  2. Add spinach and stir until wilted.
  3. Season with black pepper and serve warm.

3. Egg & Veggie Scramble

Ingredients: Eggs, bell peppers, onions, spinach, a pinch of salt & pepper.

Benefits: Quick, easy, high in protein and micronutrients.

  1. Whisk eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Saut√© chopped vegetables, then pour eggs into the pan.
  3. Scramble until eggs are fluffy. Serve hot.

4. Greek Yogurt Parfait

Ingredients: Greek yogurt, chia seeds, berries, nuts.

Benefits: Rich in protein, healthy fats, and antioxidants.

  1. Layer Greek yogurt in a glass or bowl.
  2. Top with chia seeds, fresh berries, and a sprinkle of nuts.
  3. Chill for 5 minutes and enjoy!

1. Chickpea & Quinoa Bowl

Ingredients: Cooked quinoa, roasted chickpeas, spinach, cherry tomatoes, tahini dressing.

Benefits: Packed with plant-based protein and fiber, keeps you full for hours.

  1. Cook quinoa as per instructions and let it cool.
  2. Roast chickpeas with a little olive oil and spices.
  3. Assemble bowl with quinoa, chickpeas, veggies, and drizzle tahini dressing.

2. Tofu & Veggie Stir Fry

Ingredients: Tofu cubes, broccoli, carrots, soy sauce, sesame oil.

Benefits: Excellent protein source, full of vitamins, quick 15-minute meal.

  1. Pan-fry tofu cubes until golden.
  2. Add broccoli and carrots, stir fry for 5 minutes.
  3. Season with soy sauce & sesame oil before serving.

3. Lentil Soup

Ingredients: Red lentils, onion, garlic, carrots, vegetable broth, spices.

Benefits: Warm, comforting, and rich in protein & minerals.

  1. Saut√© onion, garlic, and carrots in a pot.
  2. Add lentils, broth, and spices; simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Blend slightly for a creamy texture or serve as is.

4. Peanut Butter Oat Smoothie

Ingredients: Oats, banana, peanut butter, almond milk, chia seeds.

Benefits: High in healthy fats and protein, great for breakfast or post-workout.

  1. Add oats, banana, peanut butter, and almond milk to blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into a glass, sprinkle chia seeds on top, and enjoy!
